Steamed Dumplings – 小籠包

Originated in Central China. A dumpling filled with soup and pork, then steamed. Generally served with vinegar.

Barbeque Pork - 叉燒

Originated in GuangDong, Southern China. Slowly cooked pork that’s marinated in soy sauce, garlic, sugar, and other ingredients.

Egg Waffle – 雞蛋仔

Originated in Hong Kong. It’s a type of street food made with flour, butter and eggs. Generally served with sugar, peanut butter, and condensed milk.

Rice Noodles – 腸粉

Originated in GuangDong, Southern China. Thin sheets of steamed rice mixture wrapped around meats such as beef, pork, shrimps, etc.

Curry fish ball - 咖哩魚蛋

Originated in Hong Kong. Common street food, deep fried fish balls, then boiled in thin curry.

Egg tart – 蛋撻

Originated in GuangZhou. Common street food, a baked tart filled with egg custard.

ShuMai – 燒賣

Originated in Southern China, Steamed glutenous rice mixture with fish, wrapped in a yellow dumpling.
